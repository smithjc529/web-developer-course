A few facts about my favorite author:
Samuel Clemens

Primarily a nineteenth-century author, also known by his
pseudonym, Mark Twain, he has written hundreds of short-stories,
multiple essays, and many novels.

A few random facts

  • Born: November 30th, 1835.
  • Died: April 21st, 1910.
  • Worked as a riverboat pilot, journalist, inventor, and lecturer.
  • He is probably most well-known for his work: The Adventures of Tom Sawyer

A Few of his other works are:

  1. A Connecticut Yankee in King Aurthur's Court
  2. The Celebrated Jumping Frog of Calaveras County
  3. To Raise Poultry
